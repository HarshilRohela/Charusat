ABOUT CHARUSAT

Charotar University of Science and Technology (CHARUSAT) is established under the Gujarat Act No. 8 of 1995, Government of Gujarat. University Grants Commission (UGC) has empowered CHARUSAT to award Degrees under Section 22 of UGC Act 1956. Earlier campus was known as Education Campus Changa until 2009.The Gujarat Government has granted "Centre of Excellence" to the University. CHARUSAT was accredited with grade A+ by NAAC in 2022. It's ranked 22nd best university in whole India.

Our services
RADIOLOGY

The Department of Radiology provides a full range of radiological services related to CT Ultrasound, Fluoroscopy, DEXA, X-Rays and basic intervention procedures. The department consists of highly skilled technicians and radiologists who are dedicated to provide 24×7 patient care.

ICU & OPD

In this hospital, there are 90 Consultation rooms, with over 20 specialities and well-experienced doctors. The Neat and clean waiting area is also available for a patient’s relatives. We have 36 deluxe, 4 suites, 76 Double and Triple sharing rooms, as well as 1300 General Beds available where the patient’s relative can also stay and keep their belongings too.

CLINICAL-LABORATORY

Over the years, CHARUSAT Hospital Pathological department has gained expertise and abilities which are unmatched at ensuring that high quality of services from customer care to personal attention to the details of the individual findings.

EMERGENCY

At CHARUSAT Hospital, has 24 hours emergency service department that takes care of patients round the clock. Emergency medical care unit is headed by senior experience doctor. MESSAGE

Dear all, CHARUSAT HOSPITAL: A STEP CLOSER TO A BETTER WELL- BEING AND NEW LIFE. CHARUSAT healthcare and Research Foundation (CHRF) is a not-for-profit organization established to work towards its mission of ameliorations in human lives, by providing quality healthcare services at an affordable rate to the community in rural areas. It gives me a great pleasure to say that it is first standalone charitable multispecialty hospital in the whole area of Charotar region of Gujarat to be awarded accreditation from National Accreditation Board for Hospital and Healthcare Providers (NABH).

Our journey began in the year 2015. CHARUSAT Hospital was built in lush green campus at Changa Village, Dist. Anand Gujarat. It has started its operation with 100 beds capacity with 24×7 Accident & Emergency services, ICU, NICU, Well- Equipped Operation Theaters, Blood Bank facilities and with a team of doctors and support staff. It serves the patients with the motto of “Care with Compassion”. CHARUSAT Hospital is also a Teaching Hospital and it provides training to the students of institutes of Nursing, Physiotherapy, Pharmacy as well as Paramedical Sciences.
