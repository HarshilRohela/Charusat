ABOUT CHARUSAT

Charotar University of Science and Technology (CHARUSAT) is established under the Gujarat Act No. 8 of 1995, Government of Gujarat. University Grants Commission (UGC) has empowered CHARUSAT to award Degrees under Section 22 of UGC Act 1956. Earlier campus was known as Education Campus Changa until 2009.The Gujarat Government has granted "Centre of Excellence" to the University. CHARUSAT was accredited with grade A+ by NAAC in 2022. It's ranked 22nd best university in whole India.

OVERVIEW

To serve the society and community in a better way, CHARUSAT Healthcare and Research Foundation (CHRF) a not-for-profit organization has established a world-class multispecialty CHARUSAT hospital, holding motto of ‘Care with Compassion’. CHARUSAT hospital has been conducting various activities in the areas of Child Care, Woman Care, Healthcare related to lifestyle diseases and more. It also undertakes awareness programs and conducting camps in nearby villages for providing effective & timely healthcare facilities to the community. CHARUSAT Hospital is also providing clinical training and quality healthcare education to the students in the area of Physiotherapy, Nursing, Pharmacy, and Paramedical sciences. It is functional since 2015, with 100 bed capacity(expandable up to 450 beds) with a team of doctors and supporting staff. Presently, it is giving treatment to the patients at an affordable rate. CHARUSAT hospital is designed with emphasis on excellence in clinical services, diagnostic facilities and research. It has most modern operation theatres, ICU, NICU, Out Patient Department (OPD), 24 hours emergency services, Blood bank and well-equipped laboratory facilities.

VISION

To become a premiere organization by providing hi-tech, high quality healthcare services by research and innovations.

To work towards the amelioration of human lives by providing healthcare services to all the strata of the society by establishing world class hospitals, satellite clinics and training centers.

OBJECTIVE

To make available affordable Preventive and Curative Healthcare service to all classes of Community without discrimination of caste, creed, religion race or region through establishment of Hospitals, Dispensaries, Health centers, Diagnostic labs, etc.

To participate in the initiatives of the Government and other relevant synergetic organizations regarding healthcare, population welfare, vaccination, emergency medical services, healthcare awareness.

To undertake surveys, awareness programs, and outreach programs for healthcare amelioration of community.

To make use of prevalent & innovative technologies in delivering healthcare services.

To undertake research & development in the areas of healthcare & allied services.

To organize seminars, lectures, training programs, need based certification programs for healthcare field and other such programs for strengthening healthcare systems of society.

To disseminate knowledge and spread awareness regarding preventive care, hygiene, dietary habits and nutrition intake and other such are as related to healthcare.

To publish books, magazines, newsletters and other such literature and to use all means of communication using latest technologies to spread knowledge & information in any field with which the Foundation is concerned.
